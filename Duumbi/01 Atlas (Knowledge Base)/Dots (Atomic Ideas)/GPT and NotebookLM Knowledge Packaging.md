# GPT and NotebookLM Knowledge Packaging

## Summary

Knowledge packaging is the selection of a small, coherent active source set for custom GPTs and NotebookLM. The package should explain DUUMBI without pulling in archived execution history.

## Why it matters

GPTs and NotebookLM answer from provided instructions and sources. Too many stale or overlapping files reduce reliability and make business or development decisions harder.
